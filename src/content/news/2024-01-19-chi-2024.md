Two papers (**OmniActions** & **Human I/O**) got accepted by [CHI 2024](https://chi2024.acm.org/) 🎉. [OmniActions](/assets/pdf/CHI_2024_OmniActions_Camera_Ready.pdf) is from my Meta internship on user intent prediction in pervasive AR scenarios leveraging LLMs and VLMs. [Human I/O](https://liubruce.me/assets/pdf/liu2024humanio.pdf) is on identifying situational impairments using egocentric videos leveraging reasoning of LLMs.
